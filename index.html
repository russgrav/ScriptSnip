About Audio Condenser

This tool extracts speech audio from video files based on subtitle timing, creating condensed audio perfect for language immersion learning.

How it works:

  1. Upload video files and corresponding subtitle files
  2. The tool analyzes subtitle timing to identify speech periods
  3. Audio is extracted and segmented based on these periods
  4. Segments are concatenated into condensed audio
  5. Download the result for efficient language practice

Supported formats:

Video: MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV, WebM, MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A, AAC

Subtitles: SRT, ASS, SSA, VTT

Output: MP3, FLAC, WAV, AAC, OGG

Privacy:

All processing happens locally in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server.
